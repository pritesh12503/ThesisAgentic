DOCUMENT: MEGHALAYA STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Meghalaya

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: Limited procurement. Subsistence farming dominates.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 10
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00

Maize: Important food crop. Limited procurement.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 8

Ginger: Meghalaya is India's largest ginger-producing state. No MSP.
  note: Meghalaya ginger is premium quality. Market-driven price. State promotes GI tag.

Turmeric: Important commercial crop. No MSP.
  note: Market-driven. Meghalaya turmeric commands premium in domestic and export markets.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: MEGHALAYA STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Chief Minister's Comprehensive Farming Scheme (CMCFS):
  Input assistance for small hill farmers
  Seeds, fertilizers at subsidized rates

Meghalaya Organic Mission:
  Promotes organic certification for ginger, turmeric, and vegetables
  Premium market linkage for organic-certified produce

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0
