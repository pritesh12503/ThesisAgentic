DOCUMENT: KERALA STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Kerala

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: Kerala pays significant state bonus over central MSP. Major procurement state.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 6.37
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 90
  effective_msp_per_kg: 29.37
  note: Kerala Civil Supplies Corporation procures at central MSP + state bonus.
  source: Kerala Agriculture Department, Kharif 2023-24 (Rs 6.37/kg bonus).
  note2: Bonus may change based on budget. Central CACP recommends states reduce bonuses
         but Kerala continues to provide income support to paddy farmers.

Coconut: Kerala Coconut Development Board support. No MSP but floor price intervention.
  state_support_note: Kerala Coconut Development Board provides Rs 10/kg minimum price support
    for copra when market falls below floor.
  effective_floor_per_kg: 10 (for dry copra/copra equivalent)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: KERALA STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Kerala Karshaka Kshesha Nidhi (Farmer Welfare Fund):
  Pension and social security for farmers
  Not a direct crop income support.

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0

Vegetable and Fruit Promotion Council Keralam (VFPCK):
  Connects vegetable farmers to institutional buyers (supermarkets, hospitals)
  Provides 5-15% price premium over APMC for enrolled farmers.
  Crops: vegetables, fruits, banana, tapioca

================================================================================
SECTION 3: PMFBY
================================================================================
PMFBY: 2% Kharif, 1.5% Rabi. Standard national rates apply.
pmfby_state_reliability: moderate
