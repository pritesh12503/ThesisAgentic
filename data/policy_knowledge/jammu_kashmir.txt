SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice (Basmati): J&K grows premium Basmati. No separate MSP — central MSP applies.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 25
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00
  note: Basmati from J&K commands premium market price (Rs 35-80/kg) above central MSP.

Maize: Important Kharif crop in Jammu division.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 15
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

Apple: J&K is India's second largest apple producer after HP. No MSP.
  note: Apple is largest horticultural crop. Market-driven price. J&K earns significant revenue from apple exports.

Saffron: Pampore, J&K produces world's premium saffron. No MSP.
  note: GI tag awarded. Price Rs 250,000-500,000/kg. Spices Board monitors quality.
