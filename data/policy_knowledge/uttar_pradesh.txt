SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Wheat: UP is major wheat state but procurement efficiency lower than Punjab/Haryana.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 45
  effective_msp_per_kg: 21.75

Rice: Low procurement efficiency despite being large producer.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 8
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00
  note: Only ~8% of UP rice reaches MSP procurement. Most sold in open market below MSP.

Sugarcane: UP is India's largest sugarcane state. State Advised Price (SAP) is ABOVE FRP.
  state_advised_price_per_kg: 3.65
  frp_per_kg: 3.40
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0.25
  effective_msp_per_kg: 3.65
  note: UP SAP Rs 365/quintal vs FRP Rs 340/quintal. SAP is binding in UP.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 95 (nearly all cane sold to mills)

Arhar/Tur: Low procurement efficiency.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 10
  effective_msp_per_kg: 70.00
