================================================================================
SECTION 2: WEST BENGAL STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Krishak Bandhu (Farmer Friend):
  Rs 10000 per year per acre (Rs 5000/season) for land-owning farmers
  Minimum Rs 2000/year for marginal farmers with < 1 acre
  krishak_bandhu_per_ha_per_season: 12355
  additional_income_support_per_season: 10355 (above PM-KISAN; WB chose separate scheme)
  note: West Bengal initially did not implement PM-KISAN, opting for Krishak Bandhu instead.
        Check current status — both may now be available.

Sufal Bangla: direct-to-consumer vegetable markets. Farmers get better prices.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: PMFBY
================================================================================
West Bengal opted out of PMFBY. Has its own state crop insurance scheme.
  state_insurance_scheme: Bangla Shasya Bima
  farmer_premium: 1% (lower than national PMFBY rates)
  pmfby_state_reliability: good (own scheme)
