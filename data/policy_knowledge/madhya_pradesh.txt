SECTION 2: MADHYA PRADESH STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Mukhyamantri Krishak Samridhi Yojana:
  Bonus incentive for farmers selling at MSP procurement centres
  Additional Rs 265/quintal for wheat sold to government procurement

Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojana (Price Deficiency Payment):
  For crops where market price falls below MSP
  Government pays the difference directly to farmer's bank account
  Active for: soyabean, onion, garlic, groundnut, arhar

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — fully implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0
