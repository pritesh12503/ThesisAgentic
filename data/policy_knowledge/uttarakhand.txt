================================================================================
SECTION 2: UTTARAKHAND STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Mukhyamantri Krishak Sashaktikaran Yojana:
  Financial assistance for small hill farmers
  Input subsidy and agricultural equipment support

Parvatiya Kisan Puruskar: award and incentive for hill farmers adopting organic farming.

Uttarakhand Organic Farming Policy:
  State promotes organic certification for hill crops (apples, herbs, spices, rice)
  Organic certified produce typically commands 20-40% premium in market

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0
