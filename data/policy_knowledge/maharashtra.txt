DOCUMENT: MAHARASHTRA STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Maharashtra

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: No fixed state bonus currently. Maharashtra announces paddy bonus periodically.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 40
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00
  note: Maharashtra CM has announced paddy bonus in winter session historically. Verify current season.

Soyabean: Central MSP Rs 49.92/kg. Maharashtra is top soyabean state.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 25
  effective_msp_per_kg: 49.92
  note: NAFED procurement triggered only when market price falls below MSP.

Cotton: Central MSP Rs 70.21/kg (medium staple). CCI procurement active in Vidarbha.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 35
  effective_msp_per_kg: 70.21

Arhar/Tur: Central MSP Rs 70.00/kg. Maharashtra is major tur-producing state.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 30
  effective_msp_per_kg: 70.00
  note: 100% procurement guarantee under Budget 2025. NAFED and NCCF active.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: MAHARASHTRA STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Namo Shetkari Mahasanman Nidhi:
  Rs 6000 per year additional income support (on top of PM-KISAN)
  Total income support = PM-KISAN Rs 6000 + Namo Shetkari Rs 6000 = Rs 12000/year
  Per season: Rs 4000 (Rs 2000 PM-KISAN + Rs 2000 Namo Shetkari)
  additional_income_support_per_season: 2000

Maharashtra Shetkari Sathi Yojana:
  Zero percent interest crop loans for small and marginal farmers
  Loan up to Rs 3 lakh at 0% interest if repaid on time

Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan:
  Water conservation works in drought-prone districts (Marathwada, Vidarbha)
  Reduces input costs through improved irrigation access

PMJDY + DBT: direct subsidy transfers active.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: PMFBY
================================================================================
PMFBY implemented. Standard rates: 2% Kharif, 1.5% Rabi.
Maharashtra has had delays in releasing state share of premium in some years.
  pmfby_state_reliability: moderate
  note: Verify premium payment status for current season at pmfby.gov.in

================================================================================
SECTION 4: INPUT SUBSIDIES
================================================================================
power_subsidy_per_ha: 3000 (partial electricity subsidy for pump sets)
Fertilizer: national DBT subsidy applies.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: DISTRESS REGIONS
================================================================================
Vidarbha and Marathwada are designated distress regions.
Additional relief packages announced periodically — check Maharashtra Agriculture Department.
