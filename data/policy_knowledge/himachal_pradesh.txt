DOCUMENT: HIMACHAL PRADESH STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Himachal Pradesh

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Wheat: HP procures wheat. Limited plains area for procurement.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 25
  effective_msp_per_kg: 21.75

Maize: Major Kharif crop in HP hills.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 15
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

Apple: No MSP. HP is India's largest apple-producing state.
  note: Market-driven price. Apple mandis in Shimla and Kullu. Cold storage available.
  state_floor_price_note: HP government announces indicative floor price for apple each season.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 0

================================================================================
SECTION 2: HIMACHAL PRADESH STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
HP Mukhyamantri Khet Sanrakshan Yojana:
  Subsidized fencing for crop protection from wild animals
  Up to 50% subsidy on fencing material

Universal Health Protection Scheme: crop damage insurance for horticulture crops.

Horticulture Development Programme:
  Subsidy on cold storage, pack houses, and reefer transport for apple growers
  Reduces post-harvest losses significantly

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0

================================================================================
SECTION 3: PMFBY
================================================================================
PMFBY active in lower plains of HP. Limited in high altitude areas.
pmfby_state_reliability: moderate
