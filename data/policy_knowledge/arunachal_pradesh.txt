STATE: Arunachal Pradesh

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: Important food crop in valleys. Very limited procurement.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 8
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00

Maize: Valley crop. Limited procurement.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 5
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

Kiwi, Apple, Orange: Major horticulture crops. No MSP.
  note: Arunachal produces Kiwi (India's largest producer), large cardamom, and cinnamon.
  Market-driven prices. NHM supports post-harvest infrastructure.

  Subsidy for orchard establishment (apple, kiwi, orange)
