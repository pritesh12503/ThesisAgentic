================================================================================
SECTION 2: HARYANA STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Mera Pani Meri Virasat (My Water My Heritage):
  Rs 7000 per acre incentive for farmers who shift from paddy to non-paddy crops
  Eligible alternative crops: maize, arhar, moong, urad, cotton, sunflower
  Applicable in blocks where groundwater depth > 40 feet

Bhavantar Bharpai Yojana (Haryana):
  Price deficiency payment for horticulture crops when market price falls below notified rate
  Covers: potato, tomato, onion, cauliflower, brinjal, cabbage, garlic
  Payment = (notified_rate - actual_sale_price) × quantity sold at registered mandi

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — fully implemented.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: PMFBY
================================================================================
PMFBY implemented. Standard premium rates: 2% Kharif, 1.5% Rabi.
Haryana pays state premium share on time.
