STATE: Manipur

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: Manipur is a rice-producing state. Limited procurement infrastructure.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 15
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00

Maize: Important crop in hill districts.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 10
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

Black Rice (Chakhao): Indigenous variety. Premium market price. No MSP.
  note: Chakhao black rice commands Rs 150-300/kg in premium/export markets.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: MANIPUR STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Chief Minister's Agricultural Mechanisation Scheme:
  Subsidy on farm machinery (tractors, power tillers) for small farmers

Manipur Organic Mission:
  State promotes organic certification for hill tribal farmers
  Organic produce linked to premium buyers and export markets

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented but with some registration challenges.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0
