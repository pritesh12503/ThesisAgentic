DOCUMENT: NAGALAND STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Nagaland

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Rice: Primary food crop. Very limited procurement due to jhum cultivation system.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 5
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00

Maize: Important in settled areas. Limited procurement.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 8
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

King Chilli (Raja Mircha / Bhut Jolokia): Nagaland's premium commercial crop. No MSP.
  note: World's hottest chilli. GI tag awarded. Commands Rs 200-500/kg in premium markets.

SECTION 2: NAGALAND STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Nagaland Organic Mission:
  Promotes organic certification for all major crops
  Target: entire Nagaland state to be certified organic by 2025

Chief Minister's Integrated Agriculture Mission:
  Convergence scheme for agricultural development
  Focus on integrated farming systems for hill tribes

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0
