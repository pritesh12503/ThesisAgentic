DOCUMENT: SIKKIM STATE AGRICULTURAL POLICY 2024-25
STATE: Sikkim

================================================================================
SECTION 1: STATE BONUS OVER CENTRAL MSP
================================================================================
Maize: Important hill crop. Limited procurement.
  state_bonus_per_kg: 0
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 10
  effective_msp_per_kg: 22.25

Rice: Valley areas only. Very limited surplus.
  procurement_efficiency_pct: 8
  effective_msp_per_kg: 23.00

Large Cardamom: Sikkim is India's largest large cardamom producer. No MSP.
  note: Spices Board monitors market. Premium export product. Rs 800-2000/kg range.

Organic Vegetables: Sikkim is India's first fully organic state (2016).
  note: All crops in Sikkim are organically certified. Organic premium applies to all produce.
  organic_premium_pct: 30

================================================================================
SECTION 2: SIKKIM STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES
================================================================================
Sikkim Organic Mission:
  State achieved 100% organic certification in 2016 (first in India)
  All farmers receive free organic certification
  Organic market linkage with premium buyers (hotels, supermarkets, export)
  organic_certification_subsidy: fully_state_funded

Chief Minister's Agriculture Stabilisation Fund:
  Compensates farmers for crop loss due to natural disasters

PM-KISAN: Rs 2000/season — implemented.
additional_income_support_per_season: 0

pmfby_state_reliability: moderate
